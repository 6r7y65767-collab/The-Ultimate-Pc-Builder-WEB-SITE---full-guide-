Overview

This site provides a PC building simulator and informational estimates for pricing and game performance. Prices and frame rates are only estimates and should not be taken as exact or guaranteed. Actual component prices, compatibility, and in-game performance vary by region, driver versions, game settings, system configuration, and other factors.

What data we collect

This site does not collect personal information by default. We do not store or transmit personal data to third parties. The simulator runs entirely in your browser using local JavaScript.

  • Automatic: Standard web server logs (if hosted) such as IP address, request timestamps and user agent may be recorded by your hosting provider — not by this app directly.
  • User-provided: Any build data you explicitly export or save (for example by downloading a JSON or copying a URL) remains on your device or in the shared URL you create.

Cookies & local storage

The site may use browser local storage to temporarily save your build while you interact with the page. No third-party tracking cookies are used by the app itself. If you host this site behind a service that injects analytics, those third-party cookies would be governed by that provider's policy.

Accuracy of prices & FPS estimates

The pricing data and FPS numbers shown in the simulator are illustrative, simplified, and based on approximate scoring values. They are provided for planning and educational purposes only. Real-world outcomes can differ because of:

  • Regional price differences, taxes, and shipping
  • Driver and OS updates that affect performance
  • Game settings (resolution, quality presets, ray tracing, DLSS/FidelityFX)
  • Background processes, cooling, thermal throttling, and overclocking

By using the simulator you acknowledge these are estimates and you should verify current prices and benchmarks before purchasing.
